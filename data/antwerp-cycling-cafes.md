# Cycling cafes & bars — Province of Antwerp

A working list of ~40 cycling-friendly cafes, bars and koffiebars in *provincie Antwerpen*, Belgium. Compiled 2026-04-24 from public web sources (Kempen tourism board, Visit Antwerpen, Wielercafes.nl, official cafe sites, openingsuren.vlaanderen, openingsurengids.be, cycling blogs).

Ranking is a best-effort popularity ordering based on cycling-community citations + brand recognition; it is not authoritative. See `README.md` for the schema, methodology and known limitations.

> ⚠️ **Hours go stale fast.** Always re-check the cafe's own site/socials before riding out. Each entry has a `last_verified` field in the JSON.

## Per-cafe highlights

A short note on each cafe's cycling angle. (For full structured fields, see `antwerp-cycling-cafes.json`.)

1. **Vitesse Coffee & Cycling** — Iconic Antwerpen cycling cafe with retro racefiets decor; Vitesse Cycling Coffee + NY bagels; rides head into Noorderkempen.
2. **Peloton de Paris** — Pioneering BE cycling cafe; concept store with own gravel apparel and custom builds.
3. **Cafe Welkom** — Historic volkscafé (since 1932) in Rik Van Looy's hometown; serves "Keizer van Hertals" blonde ale.
4. **Cafe Mombasa** — Borgerhout institution since 2011; cycling races on big screen; 100+ beers; ~75-seat terrace.
5. **Bike Project** — Cycling cafe + workshop in city center; coffee while you wait for repairs.
6. **Cofiets** — Bike repair shop with attached specialty coffee bar in Mechelen.
7. **Knooppunt 30** — Family-run fiets- en eetcafe right at fietsknooppunt 30; large terrace; Kempen base.
8. **Brug 4** — Old bridge-keeper's house turned cycling cafe at the canal; near knooppunt 67; quiet Stille Kempen setting.
9. **Sas 7** — Brasserie at the seventh canal lock; canal-side terrace on the Kempische Kanalenroute.
10. **Fietscafe De Grot** — Self-styled fietscafe + parochiehuis in Mol-Wezel; cyclists' meeting point.
11. **De Bosduif** — "Fietsers Welkom"; large terrace; great base for Grenspark De Zoom-Kalmthoutse Heide rides.
12. **Cafe De Pullman** — Brown cafe opposite Arboretum Kalmthout; next to cycling route + Kalmthout station.
13. **Trapke Op** — Established broodjeszaak; classic early stop in Noorderkempen.
14. **Patisserie-Koffiebar Meeussen** — Bakery + koffiebar; Noorderkempen cyclist stop on the Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" list.
15. **Baalse Hei** — 36-ha 4-star camping with taverne; surrounded by signposted cycle network; ~50 themed routes nearby.
16. **Klein Engelandhoeve** — Visitor centre with cafe right at the Bels Lijntje cycle path through Turnhouts Vennengebied.
17. **Brasserie Stadspark** — Brasserie in Turnhout's stadspark; classic city stop on Kempen-edge rides.
18. **Cafe Leffe (Hotel Terminus)** — On historic Grote Markt; meals with Leffe + regional Turnhout "steppegrass" beers.
19. **Cafe Chaos** — City cafe on the Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" Turnhout cluster.
20. **Bon-Bon Jour** — Day-cafe in Turnhout centre on the Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" Turnhout cluster.
21. **Holstein Hoeve-ijs** — Farm ice cream from own dairy; terrace with sandbox + trampoline; classic post-ride stop.
22. **Cafe Berkemus** — Cycling cafe in Noorderwijk near Herentals; Sunday all-day opening = popular post-ride destination.
23. **Kinderboerderij De Lentehei** — Children's farm with cafe; fietsknooppunt 19 alongside.
24. **Torenhof IJskafee** — Bakery + ice cream cafe in Tongerlo; e-bike charging available; route info on garden banner.
25. **Corsendonk De Linde** — 3-star hotel in 4 outbuildings with on-site lounge; rural Kempen setting.
26. **Theehuisje De Wouwershoeve** — Restored farmhouse tea house with own organic produce; pick-your-own garden.
27. **De Wielredders** — Werkmmaat sustainability project: second-hand bike sales + repair + lunch and coffee bar.
28. **Bizarr Lier** — Bakery/patisserie on the Lier ramparts cycle route, between knooppunten 9 and 30.
29. **Fika foodbar Lier** — Hip Swedish-style breakfast/lunch foodbar; popular Lier cyclist meeting point.
30. **Baristik** — Coffee bar on Berlaar's Markt; daily breakfast and lunch; early-opening cyclist favourite.
31. **Den Achtersten Dries** — Farm ice cream + terrace; seasonal favourite on Land van Playsantien rides.
32. **Zandwich** — Sandwich shop on the Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" list; quick lunch stop.
33. **Den Heibaard** — Trout-pond + taverne; classic croque + fish soup; popular cyclist + walker rest stop.
34. **Taverne 't Dorp** — Bicycle-friendly taverne with accommodation; Kempen routes base.
35. **Cafe De Zwaluw** — Cozy village cafe in Loenhout; 50+ beers; on Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" list.
36. **Het Boerenijsje** — Farm ice cream, pancakes, waffles; on Kempen "Fietsers Welkom" list.
37. **Cafe Het Wiel** — Bornem cafe with cycling-themed name; hosts cycling-related events; Scheldeland.
38. **Cultuurcafe Bornem (Ter Dilft)** — Cultuurcafe attached to Ter Dilft cultural centre; popular Scheldeland cyclist stop.
39. **Wijnegemse Pijl** — Lower-confidence: regional cycling event hub in Wijnegem (not a fixed cafe). Replace with a verified single-cafe entry if you find a better local option.
